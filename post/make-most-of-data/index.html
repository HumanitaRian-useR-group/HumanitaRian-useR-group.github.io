How to make the most of our data?

May 16, 2017 Ricardo Bion

1 minutes read


An excellent video on how to build a data science culture within an organisation

AirBnB particpated to the recent R user conference in NYC in May 2017.

The video posted here and this blog post presents 3 important principles:

  • Develop packages to mainstream analysis workflow.

  • Promote education & contributions within the organisation

  • Ensure reproducibility.
